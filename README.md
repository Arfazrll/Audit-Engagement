## FINDINGS SUMMARY & RISK CATEGORIZATION

Key observations from the engagement are categorized by risk severity:

- **Critical Priority:** Findings requiring immediate management attention and remediation
- **High Priority:** Significant control deficiencies requiring formalized remediation plans
- **Medium Priority:** Control design improvements with defined implementation timeline
- **Low Priority:** Recommendations for operational enhancements and process optimization
